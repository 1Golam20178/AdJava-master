Code of Conduct

Preamble

This Code of Conduct is formulated to provide a guideline to the students regarding their behavior and personal conduct. Students are expected to comply with the requirements set in this Code of Conduct. Violation of this conduct shall invoke disciplinary processes as outlined in this document.

With a view to ensuring the general ethical responsibilities and obligations of all the students towards their class-mates, friends, teachers, officers, seniors, juniors & staff of the University, the following have been adopted as the prescribed Code of Conduct for the students:

Academic Code of Conduct

1. Student's Conduct with the Faculty Member:

  • They have to be very polite and modest in manners with their teachers.
  • They must keep their mobile/cell phone switched off while attending the classes.
  • They must enter and leave the classroom with the permission of the teacher.
  • They must follow the instructions and guidance of the concerned teacher regarding matters related to the course.
  • Without prior permission, they are not allowed to visit their teacher's office/chamber beyond the consultation hours fixed by the concerned course teacher.
  • The CRs (Class Representatives) are allowed to meet their teachers regularly to discuss matters related to class administration.
  • They must follow the instruction of their teachers even outside the campus, i.e., in the annual picnic, excursion, study tour, industry/court visit & similar programs organized officially by department, school, and/or university.
  • They must not tell lies to escape punishment for their misdeeds.
  • They must not produce any false and fabricated document to their teachers to avail of any benefit, i.e., forged documents of medical certificate.

2. Conduct with the Officers of the University:

  • All officers of this university should also be equally respected by all students.
  • The Identification Cards must be carried by all students at all times and hung it around his/her neck while inside the Campus.
  • The students must seek prior permission of the concerned officers before entering into their office. It is a matter of courtesy.
  • They must follow the instructions of the librarian while using library facilities.
  • They must follow the instructions of the IT expert/Lab Assistant while using the computer lab.
  • They are not allowed to enter any office room of any campus of SEU, where entrance is restricted for the students except with prior permission of the authorized person.
